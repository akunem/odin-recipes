Coconut Pie

This coconut pie recipe includes a homemade custardy filling with lots of flaked coconut and vanilla for a sweet and delicious dessert.

Ingredients

  • Sugar: This coconut pie recipe starts with a cup of white sugar.
  • Flour: All-purpose flour helps thicken the filling.
  • Eggs: Three eggs add moisture and help bind the filling together.
  • Butter: A stick of softened butter lends moisture and richness.
  • Milk: A cup of milk helps create the perfect sweet coconut filling.
  • Vanilla: Vanilla extract takes the flavor up a notch.
  • Coconut: Youâ€™ll need a cup of flaked coconut.
  • Pie crust: Use a store-bought or make your own at home.

Steps

  1. Mix the dry ingredients together, then beat in the eggs and butter.
  2. Stir in the milk and vanilla, then fold in the coconut flakes.
  3. Pour the filling into the pie crust and bake.

Notes

Store the coconut pie, covered tightly or in an airtight container, in the refrigerator for up to four days. You can freeze this coconut pie for up to three months. Thaw in the refrigerator overnight.
